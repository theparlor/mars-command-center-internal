Coaching Phase Rationale

With foundational training complete, we're transitioning into embedded coaching and discovery. Over the next two weeks, our coaches will be observing, learning, and building trust with each team before introducing hands-on guidance. This is intentional — the operational topics we need to address (estimation, story writing, backlog management) vary significantly from team to team. Training on these topics now, before we understand each team's unique workflows, would risk creating six different implementations of the same concept — the opposite of the "single pane of glass" outcome we're building toward.

Value to Be Delivered These Two Weeks

Real-world understanding of how each team actually operates day-to-day
Identification of quick wins we can deliver early to build momentum
Baseline documentation to measure improvement against over 20 weeks
Relationship foundation that makes future coaching recommendations stick
