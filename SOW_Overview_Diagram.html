Agile Transformation — Scope of Work

20-Week Engagement · M.A.R.S. Platforms · One Cohort
Subaru of America × Turnberry Solutions

Transformation Intent

SOA and Turnberry are partnering to begin an Agile Transformation intended to improve overall speed of delivery, increase the efficiency of platform team delivery, and create transparency between teams and leaders. All delivery will be achieved through doing the actual work side-by-side with the platform teams and leaders.

Focus 1

Training & Toolkit

Custom Agile curriculum delivered to teams, launching new routines

In-Scope Services

  • Custom toolkit (SharePoint / Mural / LMS)
  • Agile & Scrum Training (up to 25 members, 2 weeks)
  • Communications & OCM

Training Modules

  • Agile Mindset & Continuous Improvement
  • Product Mindset & Product Discovery
  • Scrum and Kanban Fundamentals
  • Navigating Change
  • Achieving Flow (Batch Size & Self-Organization)
  • Writing Effective User Stories
  • Challenging Assumptions (Personas & Hypotheses)
  • Aligning on Value (Story Maps & MVP)
  • Customer Outcomes & Leading/Lagging Indicators

Key Deliverables

  • Training recordings, toolkit, and playbook
  • Team DoR, DoD, and delivery metrics standards
  • Strategic scorecards and roadmap templates
Focus 2

Coaching

Tailored coaching dovetailing with training, standardized and customized

In-Scope Services

  • Day-to-day team coaching (12-15 per coach)
  • Platform Leader coaching (weekly 1:1s)
  • Increment Planning facilitation (x2)
  • Communications & OCM

Key Deliverables

  • 30-60-90-day coaching plans per team
  • Platform leader coaching sessions
  • OCM & Communications Plan
  • Bi-weekly status reporting
  • Increment Planning materials (prep, facilitation guide, exit)

Coaching Approach

  • Assess team maturity at start
  • Standardized foundation + customized mentoring
  • Side-by-side delivery with teams
Focus 3

JIRA & Metrics

Common JIRA config, industry-standard metrics, baseline outcomes

JIRA Configuration

  • Best practices: project setup, fields, workflow
  • JIRA Playbook with work classification
  • Issue hierarchy: Initiative > Epic > Story > Task > Bug
  • Fix Versions, Components, Labels standards
  • Team training on standardized usage

JIRA Reporting

  • Out-of-the-box reporting config & training
  • Dashboards: leader level and team level
  • JIRA Add-On recommendations

Team Scorecards

  • Velocity
  • Predictability
  • Cycle Time
  • Defect Leakage & Density
  • Team Health
