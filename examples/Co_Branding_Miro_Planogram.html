Anti-Patterns — What NOT to Do

Dueling Logos Two logo images side-by-side in every footer. Creates visual competition.
Logo Soup More than 2 logos on one slide. Third parties get text only.
Equal Sizing Same-size logos signal ambiguity. Subaru should always be larger.
Logo as Decoration Data slides earn 100% real estate for content. No logos needed.
Footer Logo Wars Both as images in footer. One image + one text = clean.
